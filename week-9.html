Week 9 - Memes pandemic

Week nine was a very busy week for all of us. The front-end team converted most of their Figma sketches to code. Other than this, Gagan and Jim worked on making our web application scalable. They compared costs and service models of different popular web services in the market, and decided to go with Amazon Web Services. After this, they wrote several bash scripts to automate the process of transferring our web applications and automatically scale it with increase in demand. Naman worked on password authentication during this week with Stirling & Jim.

The simulations team worked on creating the environment for the robot. So far they worked on accuracy and getting things right, now they're moving into the aesthetics and making things look pretty! Other than this, this week our team heavily contributed to Inf Meme FB group. pog.
