Week 7 - Peer Review

During this week our work focused primarily on preparing to participate in a peer review with another group. Fortunately, this group was also working with UV technology and had been experiencing similar hardware difficulties, we found this reassuring and useful as we were able to exchange usual information about potential issues with development.

The software team during this period focused primarily on implementing a Webots API that would allow us to use the same overarching controller on both hardware and simulation versions, accessing the appropriate API.

The hardware team worked closely with technicians to resolve issues with the physical robot. They installed the bump and IR sensors and conducted testing on these sensors.

We also began to shift our focus away from the development of the robot briefly in order to prepare for our demonstration by conducting quantitative analysis and polishing any features which we had implemented following the last demo.
