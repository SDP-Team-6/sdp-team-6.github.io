Week 10 - Last Demo AAA!

This week we focussed towards trying to get a working hardware prototype, refining the design of the Web UI and creating a train environment in webots. At the start of the week we discussed the possibility that the Hardware prototype might not be fixed in time for the demo and as a contingency the Software Team started working towards making a Train environment simulation for the demo video. We would use this Train environment to simulate cleaning poles PAUL inside a train carriage as this is one of the Use Cases we see for PAUL.

The Front-End team started adding content to the website so that we have a head start for the website deadline next week. Other than this, they implemented few of the accessibility settings as well. They also provided a walkthrough of how to use the website for the user guide and demo video.

The hardware team worked to resolve the hardware issues with the prototype but ran into a myriad of issues. They attended the tech-live lab session each day this week from 12-5pm but were unable to resolve all the hardware issues before the week ended. This means that we don't have a working hardware prototype for the demo next week but we hope to keep working next week and get it fixed in time for the industry day. They met with our client Steve on Thursday to discuss the hardware issues and check that he was happy with our plan to use webots footage in the video in place of hardware footage.

We had to reschedule our weekly meeting as a few of the group members were not able to attend on Thursday. We met on Saturday instead and discussed what each team had worked towards, informed the entire team about the hardware issues and the plan for getting the report done.
