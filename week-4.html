Week 4 - Demo 1 & Feedback

We started the week with working on the demo one report and video. The Project management team were making final edits to the video and the report. Everyone met up on monday to discuss what changes were required and made a quality check to see if we missed something, finally Daniel submitted the report and we all called it a day!

The team met again on tuesday to discuss wednesday's meeting and had a practice Q&A session with our client & mentor. The Front-end team discussed usability & accessibility issues, the hardware & the software team brainstormed different edge cases in a practical setting.

On wednesday, we finally met with our client Steve and discussed our next steps, and it revolved around the ethics and accessibility issues of our robot. It gave us a new perspective on how we can improve upon our next demo.

Finally, our team met once again on thursday to discuss our tasks for the next few weeks. Project managers made new tasks on trello, and other team members joined the tasks they were interested in. Software team scheduled a meeting with the front-end team to work on the software part. After this, everyone independently worked on their part.
