Week 1 - Title Name

This week we focused on getting the hardware prototype ready for the demo and starting work on a Web UI.

Members of the Front-End team attended the Usability workshop to help them better understand the features they should add to the Website and Web-based User Interface. They drew up wireframes for the Web UI and received feedback on these from the Usability Expert.

The hardware team worked on the UV module which was successfully installed. They tested the climbing of the robot with the UV module attached to ensure that the added weight did not impact this. They encountered some issues with the LED which they intend to fix next week after the demo. We had our weekly meeting on Thursday, we heard the progress of each team, discussed the progress towards writing the report and ideas for the demo video.
