Pole Automation Using Ultra-Violet Light

Paul is a pole climbing robot designed to disinfect poles in public transport via a band of low wavelength ultraviolet radiation (UVC).

Over the past year, public transport companies have significantly increased the amount of money they spend on sanitisation and yet high-touch point spaces, are often missed, spreading deadly flu and viruses. With PAUL we aim to target these virus hotspots and reduce the accumulation of harmful microbes.
