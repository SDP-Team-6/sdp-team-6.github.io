Week 2 - Title

Over the weekend we decided that we wanted to change our project idea after we decided our original idea wasn't feasible. At the first meeting of the week on Monday we decided on a new robot idea which would be used to disinfect poles on public transport using ultraviolet light. We decided to call this robot PAUL (Pole Automaton using Ultraviolet Light). On Tuesday we met with both Garry and Steve (our client) to discuss the feasibility of our new idea, they were both happy with the new idea.

Later on Tuesday, we met as a group and allocated sections of the Project Plan for each group member to work on. We also decided that we wanted to organise our group into sub-teams which focussed on working areas of our project.

On Thursday we had a brainstorming meeting as a team to work on the Project Plan as a team. This allowed members to discuss aspects of the Project Plan they were unsure of and ensured that the Project Plan was a good reflection of what the entire group thought was feasible.
