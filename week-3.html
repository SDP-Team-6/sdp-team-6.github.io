Week 3 - Project Plan

The main focus of this week of development was preparing our software simulation for our upcoming demonstration. We used our project plan and tasks decided upon therein to highlight our priority areas. We had a meeting to discuss what subtasks would be allocated to each person in the simulation team.

By Friday, we had successfully managed to implement a rudimentary robot design that was able to climb the poll as we had envisioned; additionally managing to achieve successful implementation of distance sensors by Sunday.

Our front-end team during this time also ensured that the skeleton of the website had been prepared and was undertaking research regarding best practices for implementation of the web UI. The team undertook individual study to familiarise themselves with relevant technologies.

Our hardware team leveraged this time to translate our ideas into a working prototype. Creating models and meeting with experts to facilitate this.
