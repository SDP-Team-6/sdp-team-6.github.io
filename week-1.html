Week 1 - SDP Begins

This is our first of our 11 blogs we've planned for this course. We took several approaches to reflect from our work and improve as we move along, and writing this blog is one of them.

This week our team attended several workshops and presentations hosted by the SDP staff and we learnt more about the logistics of the course and met a lot of people. Our team had three meetings across the week and it mainly revolved around choosing a project, learning LaTeX and WeBots. Our team worked smoothly on the logistics of everything like setting up GitHub, Doodle Polls, Teams, and the Trello Board. For the next week we plan to work on our project plan report, get to know our team, and write our project plan. This week wasn't challenging but was very demanding as we had a lot of workshops and presentations to attend. Overall, everyone was quite engaging and goals were achieved productively.
