Budget

How much does it cost?

Our system currently costs £381 to produce however, we are confident we will be able to reduce this substantially by diversifying our current suppliers and through effective negotiation. We estimate assembly to take around 4 hours of technician time at a rate of £20/h, and we aim for a markup of 20%. Therefore, currently, we aim to market our product for around £550.
