Week 6 - No Appleton :(

This week we found out we won't have access to Appleton Tower for the rest of the semester. This sucked caused most of our team was in Edinburgh, and we won't be able to meet to work on our project together nor will the hardware team will be able to work on building the prototype by themselves and had to rely on Garry for the rest of the course.

This week the software & simulations team worked on simulating vibrations using feedback from Steve, our client. The hardware team was busy with Garry to get a prototype working. This week they also developed scripts to control each feature seperately, which was later used by Naman to connect it with the buttons of the web interface.
